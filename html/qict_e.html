I rewrite QICT source to C++, and use it for Pairwise generation.
my C++ source is here.
qict.cpp


you can compile it, as followings.

(Visual C++ 2010 Express on Windows)
cl /EHsc qict.cpp

(gcc on Linux)
g++ qict.cpp -o qict
